Pumpkin Risotto

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg of kabotcha type pumpkin
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 medium onion finely chopped
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • 3 tablespoons chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 2 cups of arborio rice
  • 2 chicken broth tablets
  • 1 teaspoon of fine herbs
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 50g grated parmesan cheese
  • Copped sausage (as much as you like)
  • Pepper (as much as you like)

Video

How to make it

Peel the pumpkin and cut into small cubes. Reserve. Place the oil in a deep pan and add the chopped onion and garlic and cook over low heat until the onion and garlic are wilted, stirring constantly. Add the green onions and the chopped pumpkin and let it cook for 3 minutes, stirring constantly. Add the white wine and let it cook for 2 minutes. Add 2 cups of boiling water and the chicken broth tablets and cook until the pumpkin is slightly soft, for 10 minutes over low heat. Increase the heat and add the arborio rice. Stir well until boiling. Add the herbs, ginger and bay leaf. Lower the flame on the stove and, always stirring, add boiling water when necessary. It takes an average of 15 to 20 minutes to acquire the creamy point. Then add the butter and grated Parmesan cheese. Taste for salt and add pepper. Turn off the heat and cover the pan. Let it rest for 5 minutes. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley.
